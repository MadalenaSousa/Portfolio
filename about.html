I’m very interested in UX/UI design, in branding and other types of graphic design, game design and illustration too, sometimes. I've also done front-end web development, video production and motion design. Other than work, I also like other things, like nature (I’m a scout), books, movies, TV shows, video-games and occasional (not so successful) arts and crafts of different kinds.
