HELLO!

I’m Madalena, I’m 24 and I’m a cross-media designer based in Penacova, Coimbra.

I’ve studied design for five years, through different Universities, but ultimatly my goal was always the same: I like to make people’s lifes more pleasant. Make processes intuitive, make navigation easy and, yes, make it look pretty too.

My path until here has covered a lot of different areas within design, from fine arts to what my informatics friends call “hardcore programing”. Now, I like to be somewhere in the middle, without restricting myself. I’m very intereseted in UX/UI design, in branding and other types of graphic design, game design and illustration too, sometimes. I’m also equiped with a skillset that let’s me also do front-end web development, video production and motion design.

Other than work, I also like other things, like nature (I’m a scout), books, movies, TV shows, videogames and ocasional not so successfull arts and crafts of different kinds.
